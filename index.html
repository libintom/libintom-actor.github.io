Libin Tom is a South Asian actor based in Sydney. He practices acting and storytelling with roots from theatre, and enjoys developing complex characters with a backstory and deep personality. He has collaborated with a few theatre and production companies from Australia, and has worked in short films. He is confident in his abilities and is constantly challenging himself to grow and improve. He is excited for what the future holds and cannot wait to see where his acting journey takes him.
